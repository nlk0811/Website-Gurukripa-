Our Legacy

From Idlis to Icons

Founded by Late Mr. Narayan Shetty in 1977, Gurukripa began with humble beginnings‚Äîselling idlis at just 50 paisa. What started as a small venture has blossomed into one of Mumbai's most beloved dining institutions, cherished by generations of food lovers.
